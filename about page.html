WHAT IS LUX MUNDI CUP EVENT?

Lux Mundi's Cup is an Event happened every single year to showcase the talents and skills of the Students here in Tangub City Global College,The word LUX MUNDI came from the latin word which means LIGHT OF THE WORLD.
